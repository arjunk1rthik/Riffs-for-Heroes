Where It Goes

Every gift supports real programs

Veterans Village of San Diego provides housing, health care, and job support to veterans working to get back on their feet. Donations through Riffs for Heroes go straight to those programs — no fees, no overhead taken by us.

Prefer to give in another way? You can also support the mission by inviting Riffs for Heroes to perform at your venue or event — every show doubles as a fundraiser.
