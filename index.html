The Mission

Music that shows up
where it's needed most.

"I saw how many veterans come home from serving this country and still struggle to be seen. Riffs for Heroes is my way of showing up for them — with my guitar, and with what I raise for Veterans Village of San Diego."
What We Do

Two ways to serve those who served

🎸

Live at Veterans Village

Solo acoustic performances on-site at Veterans Village of San Diego — bringing music, company, and a break in the day to veterans working through the Village's programs, including Stand Down events.

☕

Fundraising at Local Venues

Sets at community spots like Roadies Brewery, where every dollar raised from tips and shows is donated directly to Veterans Village of San Diego to fund their housing and support services.

Why It Started

A video changed how I saw service.

Watching footage from a Veterans Village of San Diego Stand Down event, I saw veterans who'd given everything for this country still fighting to find stability, housing, and support. That stuck with me — and I wanted to do something real about it with the one thing I had: my guitar.
